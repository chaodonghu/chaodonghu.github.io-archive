Poster Presentations/Abstracts

Canadian Frailty Network Summer Student 2016 Poster - April 30, 2017 - Toronto, ON

Poster presentation and abstract that summarized my summer research in a study that aimed to investigate the effectiveness of the pharmacist intervention on the use of polypharmacy, high-risk medication use and gastrointestinal protection among frail elderly who underwent a pharmacist intervention in comparison to frail elderly who declined a pharmacist intervention at the Centre for Family Medicine Health Team. As a secondary objective, the study also aims to compare the use of emergency department and hospitalizations between the two groups. Abstracted data will be analyzed and results of the study will be used to develop a pharmacist intervention tool that can be disseminated to other primary care settings, in particular other family health teams and community pharmacies for broader use and investigation in the future.
